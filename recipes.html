Damian's Wonderful Creations

Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

For 36 Cookies:
2 sticks butter
1/2 White Sugar
1 1/2 cups brown sugar
2 eggs
11 1/2 ounces all purpose flour
1 Tablespoon Salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
3/4 teaspoon cinnamon
Chocolate Chips (use chopped semi-sweet or dark chocolate bars)
Nuts
Flaky sea salt (for garnish)

Key Lime Pie

Yields one 9-inch Key Lime Pie along with Whipped Cream:
⅔ cup key lime juice (15-30 key limes)
1 ½ packs graham crackers
40 g powdered sugar
Kosher salt
100 g unsalted butter, melted
3 egg yolks
1 large lime, zested
14 oz can sweetened condensed milk
1 pint heavy cream
2 Tablespoons sugar
Vanilla Extract

Fudgy Brownies

Yields 24 Brownies:
1/3 cup cocoa powder
1 1/2 teaspoon eespresso powder
2 ounces unsweetened chocolate, chopped
1/2 cup plus 2 Tablespoon boiling water
4 Tablespoons butter
1/2 cup plus 2 Tablespoons vegetable oil
2 large eggs plus 2 egg yolks
1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 1/2 cups granulated sugar
1 3/4 cups All-Purpose Flour
6 ounces bittersweet chocolate, chopped
